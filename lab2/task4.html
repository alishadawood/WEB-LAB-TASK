Seasons

that comes after the cold winter and before the hot

Summer

Summer is the hottest season of the year. The days are long, the sun shines brightly, and people wear light clothes to stay cool. Children enjoy their summer holidays, and many like to travel, play, or eat ice cream and cold foods.

This season also brings delicious fruits like mangoes, melons, and watermelons. Although summer is full of fun and energy, it is important to drink plenty of water and stay safe from the strong heat.
